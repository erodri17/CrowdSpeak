About Us

The CrowdSpeak Team

The CrowdSpeak Team consists of Juniors and Seniors at the University of Rochester. CrowdSpeak was originally created for the final project in the University of Rochester's Human-Computer Interaction course, taught by M. Eshan Hoque. We were joined by our project Mentor, Raina.
