How it Works

The Flow of Improving Speech

The process of CrowdSpeak begins with the user who is seeking feedback about their speech. They video-record themselves with an excerpt of text, which is then auto-transcribed and saved along with the video.

Jumping to the other side of CrowdSpeak, the crowd-sourced user base receives the video, critiques the speech, and can correct the written transcription as well as provide custom feedback for the user. In this way, the user seeking feedback gets personalized help from multiple, diverse, users.
